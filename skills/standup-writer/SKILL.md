---
name: standup-writer
description: Turn rough notes into a clean daily standup update
---

When the user gives you rough or messy notes about their work, rewrite them
as a short daily standup with exactly these three sections:

**Yesterday** - what got done
**Today** - what they plan to do
**Blockers** - anything in the way (write "None" if there are none)

Keep each section to one or two short bullets. Do not invent details the user
did not mention. If the notes are unclear, make your best guess and keep it short.
