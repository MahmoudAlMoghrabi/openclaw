**Subject: Before our AI agent workshop: two quick things to check**

Hi everyone,

We are looking forward to the hands-on session on **[DATE, TIME, LOCATION]**
where you will build and run your own AI agent. The good news: there is almost
nothing to prepare. Everything runs in your browser, and there is nothing to

Please do these small things beforehand:

**1. Have a GitHub account, and confirm Codespaces works for you.**
The workshop runs on GitHub Codespaces. Sign in and check that
https://github.com/codespaces loads for you. A **personal** account is best:
company-managed accounts often have Codespaces switched off by policy. If you
do not have an account, create one at https://github.com/signup a day or two
early. Brand-new accounts sometimes need a quick phone verification, and you do
not want to be doing that during the session.

**2. Open this link once to make sure it loads on your laptop:**
https://tinyurl.com/aiGarage
Those are the workshop slides — the same page we will all start from at the
session (the big launch button on the first slide is for workshop day; no
need to click it now). If you are on a work laptop, some company networks
block tools like this. If the page does not load, try a personal laptop, or
let us know ahead of time so we can sort it out before the day.

**3. Bring that same laptop, charged, with its charger,** and a modern browser
(Chrome, Edge, or Firefox).

**One ground rule to know in advance:** treat your workshop agent like a public
demo. Everything you type goes to a cloud AI service on a shared workshop
account, so no confidential work material, client information, or personal
data. We will cover the rest of the safety ground rules in the intro.

That is it. No prior experience with AI agents is needed, and there is nothing
to install. A passphrase we put on the screen unlocks your AI key, and we will
walk you through everything else. If you have never used GitHub before, come
10 minutes early and we will get you set up.

Anything not working, or any questions? Reply to [CONTACT EMAIL] by
[REPLY-BY DAY] so we can fix it before the session.
